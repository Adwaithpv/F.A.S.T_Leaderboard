Visual Style

Use a futuristic gaming dashboard design language inspired by esports interfaces and sci-fi HUD systems.

Design characteristics:

• Dark futuristic theme
• Neon gradients and glowing accents
• Glassmorphism / holographic panels
• Rounded futuristic cards
• Subtle grid or digital background pattern
• Smooth glow shadows and highlights

Color palette suggestion:

Background:

Deep navy / near black (#0B0F1A)

Accent colors:

Electric blue

Neon cyan

Purple

Magenta

Highlight colors for ranks:

Gold (1st place)

Silver (2nd place)

Bronze (3rd place)

Typography:

Bold futuristic display font for headings

Clean modern sans-serif for data and numbers

Large readable score numbers

Visual elements:

Rank badges

Neon progress bars

Animated glow edges

Esports style stat cards

Icon indicators for games

Leaderboard Section

Display teams ranked by total cumulative score.

Use large futuristic leaderboard cards instead of plain tables.

Each team card should contain:

• Rank number
• Rank badge icon
• Team name
• Total cumulative score
• Expand arrow icon

Larger card

Trophy icon

2nd place:

Silver glow

3rd place:

Bronze glow

Other teams:

Standard neon card style.

Interaction Design

Prototype interactions should include:

• Clicking a team card expands score breakdown
• Round tabs switch round data
• Navigation tabs switch leaderboard pages
• Hover effects with glow highlights
• Smooth expand/collapse animations

UX Goals

The experience should feel like:

• A futuristic esports tournament system
• Highly engaging and competitive
• Clear ranking hierarchy
• Visually exciting and rewarding

The leaderboard should feel alive, dynamic, and gamified, making teams excited to check their rankings.
